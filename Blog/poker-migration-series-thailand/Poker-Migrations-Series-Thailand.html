Cost of living:
Whether you're planning an extended holiday or pondering a digital nomad lifestyle, Phuket emerges as an excellent choice. Known for its awe-inspiring beaches, friendly locals, and cost-effective living expenses, the island offers a wide range of housing options. The cost of accommodation can significantly vary based on location, type of property, and season. Here's what you might expect to pay for housing on a monthly basis:

Hotels:

Extended stays at hotels in Phuket are certainly viable. A basic hotel room could cost between 15,000 to 25,000 baht per month ($450 - $750). For mid-range hotels, expect to pay between 25,000 to 50,000 baht per month ($750 - $1,500). Luxury hotels and resorts, however, may exceed 100,000 baht ($3,000) or more per month.

Serviced Apartments:

Serviced apartments are a popular choice for long stays, offering extras like cleaning services, utilities, and access to amenities such as a gym or pool. A standard serviced apartment might cost around 10,000 to 20,000 baht per month ($300 - $600), while high-end options can range from 30,000 to 60,000 baht ($900 - $1,800) or more per month.

Airbnb:

Airbnb rentals can vary widely in cost. A private room may start from around 7,000 baht ($210) per month. If you prefer a place all to yourself, prices generally start at around 15,000 baht ($450) for a simple apartment. For a luxurious villa complete with a private pool and other amenities, you might need to budget upwards of 60,000 baht ($1,800) per month.

Rental Apartments and Houses:

If you're planning a long-term stay in Phuket, renting an apartment or house may be the most cost-effective option. A basic one-bedroom apartment in a less tourist-centric area could cost around 8,000 to 15,000 baht per month ($240 - $450). In contrast, a similar apartment in a more bustling area like Patong could range between 15,000 to 30,000 baht ($450 - $900). Standalone houses or villas could start from around 20,000 baht ($600) and escalate to 100,000 baht ($3,000) or more for luxury options.

Please note, these prices are estimations and can fluctuate based on location, season, and the amenities provided. Always remember to check multiple sources, read reviews, and negotiate when possible to secure the best deal.
